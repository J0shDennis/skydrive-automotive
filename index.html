Flying cars, also known as personal air vehicles (PAVs), are vehicles designed for both ground and aerial transportation. They aim to provide a mode of transportation that combines the convenience of cars with the ability to fly. They typically use vertical takeoff and landing (VTOL) technology and are often electric to reduce emissions.
